About Us

Discover the story behind Aisha Al-Harthi Restaurant and our passion for Omani cuisine.

Aisha Al-Harthi Restaurant

We bring the authentic flavors of Oman to your table with traditional recipes passed down through generations. Located in the heart of Muscat, we offer a warm dining experience with modern convenience. Our dishes are prepared with the finest ingredients, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Oman.

Since opening in 2015, we’ve been committed to preserving Omani traditions while embracing innovation, such as our online ordering and table reservation systems.
